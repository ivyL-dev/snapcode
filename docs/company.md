# SnapCode Company Overview

SnapCode is an AI-native web and digital product company focused on interface systems, modern web experiences, and user-facing digital products.

## Positioning

SnapCode builds the visible layer of trust for modern companies: websites, landing pages, product surfaces, and digital interfaces that shape how a business is perceived online.

## Focus Areas

- AI-native web products
- Digital product interfaces
- User-facing systems
- Responsive web experiences
- Interface design systems

## Current Direction

SnapCode is building its public presence, product direction, and first-generation digital systems.
